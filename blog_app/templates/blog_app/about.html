Hello again, my curious friend!

So, you would like to know more about me? Oh, well…

20-something, born, raised and living in Slovenia. Which is, by the way, one of the prettiest countries in the whole wide world. No doubt there at all.

I live in a reasonably large apartment with lots of windows, some of them facing south and west and unfortunately none of them facing north. And north is where the mountains and snow and my heart are. Winters for me are never long enough and snowfall never really deep enough.

Besides winter and snow I also love animals, especially dogs. One of them keeps me company on a daily basis – Nessie, my little monster. Well, not so little, considering she is about my height when standing on her hind legs... which could mean either she is very tall, or I am kind of short. And I will leave you guessing the correct answer… I should probably also mention she is a certified wilderness Search and rescue dog. And she has flown in a helicopter once! Such a cool dog ( -- insert a smiley with trendy sunglasses and a smug face here -- ).

I also like photography. All the photos you see on this blog are my work. Not to say that I am a good photographer. But I try. I especially like to bring a camera with me on a hike. Slovenia has LOTS of hills and mountains and the possibilities for hiking are endless. A beautiful sunny day in the mountains with lots of snow, good friends, a dog or two and a camera – ah, my kind of days!

And with these positive images in your head I am going to finish my writing for now… but shh, I know what you are going to say! Can one really know a person by just reading a few lines about them? Wouldn’t you rather write some more lines so we can get to know you – and your cool dog - a bit better?

And the answer to that, my loves, is NO. A person is more that just a few pieces, lines on paper – pardon, computer screen. A person is much, much more than that… so I encourage you to read my blog posts and find other little pieces in there. Also animal pictures. Because why not.

Yours truly,
Maja
